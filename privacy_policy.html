Privacy Policy for SafeRec

Effective Date: 2026-02-22

Thank you for choosing SafeRec ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy details how SafeRec handles your data when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

SafeRec is explicitly designed to collect only the information necessary to provide its core functionality: recording audio and video securely and uploading it to your personal Google Drive account.

To operate effectively, SafeRec requests access to the following hardware on your device:

  • Microphone: To record audio.
  • Camera: To record video.

When you use SafeRec, the app creates audio and video recordings ("Recordings") on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

The audio and video data recorded by SafeRec are used exclusively to create the recording files you request.

We do not use this data for tracking, profiling, advertising, or compiling user behavioral metrics.

3. Storage and Data Transmission (Google Drive Integration)

SafeRec integrates directly with your personal Google Drive account to securely store your recordings.

  • Direct Upload: When a recording is completed (or in chunks during recording), the app transmits the resulting files directly from your device to a designated folder within your Google Drive.
  • No Intermediary Servers: SafeRec does not route your recordings through our servers or any third-party intermediaries. The transmission happens directly between your Android device and Google's servers.
  • Local Storage: The app may temporarily store recording data on your device's local storage (in app-specific directories) during the recording process or if a network connection is unavailable. These local files are automatically queued to be uploaded to your Google Drive and can be managed directly by you.

By authenticating with Google Drive, you are subject to Google's Privacy Policy regarding how Google stores and protects your data.

4. Google User Data Policy Compliance

The app's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • Access: The app requests the minimal scope necessary to upload, manage, and read the specific SafeRec files within your Google Drive.
  • Use: Google user data is only used to facilitate the secure upload, viewing, and deletion of your recordings.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We will not transfer, sell, or share your Google user data with any third parties under any circumstances.

5. Security

The security of your data is paramount. Because we never transmit your recordings to servers other than your authorized Google Drive, the primary security mechanisms are:

  1. The built-in security of the Android operating system to protect the temporary local files.
  2. The security provided by Google to protect data in transit to Google Drive and data at rest on Google's servers.

6. Children's Privacy

SafeRec does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Given the nature of the application (local recording to personal cloud storage), we do not have a system to identify user ages. If you are a parent and believe your child has recorded sensitive information, please review and delete the files directly from the child's device and the connected Google Drive account.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the practices of SafeRec, please reach out via our Play Store contact page or GitHub repository.
